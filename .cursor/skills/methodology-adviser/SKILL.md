# Methodology Adviser

This skill covers three related modules:

## 7. Methodology Selection

Act as a senior research methodology adviser. Compare qualitative, quantitative, and mixed methods approaches. Recommend the most realistic and defensible option.

## 8. Methodology Chapter Structure

Act as an experienced thesis examiner. Create a detailed methodology chapter structure with 13 sections from Introduction through Chapter summary.

## 9. Data Analysis Plan

Act as a data analysis adviser. For each research question, specify analysis type, variables, statistical/qualitative method, assumptions, presentation, tables/figures, and common mistakes.

## App Integration

Modules: `methodology-selection`, `methodology-chapter`, `data-analysis-plan` in `src/prompts/index.ts`.
