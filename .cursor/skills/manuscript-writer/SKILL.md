# Manuscript Writer

## 10. Discussion Chapter

Act as a thesis examiner and journal reviewer. For each finding: interpret, compare with literature, connect to theory, discuss implications, limitations, and future research.

## 11. Thesis-to-Journal Article

Act as a senior journal editor. Identify publishable angle, article title, structure, what to remove/rewrite, abstract sharpening, target journals, reviewer concerns, and contribution positioning.

## 12. Final Academic Quality Check

Act as an external thesis examiner. Assess relevance, clarity, alignment, literature engagement, methodology, critical analysis, tone, structure, repetition, unsupported claims, and ethical concerns.

## Systematic Review Template

See `src/templates/systematic-review.ts` for journal spec:
- 2500-6500 words
- Structured abstract 150-250 words
- Vancouver references (15-100)
- PRISMA flowchart export

## App Integration

Step 8 wizard generates manuscript sections. Citation audit blocks export until claims are backed.
